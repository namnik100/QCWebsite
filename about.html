About Us

The AHS Quantum Computing Club is a sub-club of the AHS Engineering and Technology Club.

The purpose of this club is to introduce the world of quantum, to high schoolers.

Together, we'll cover everything from the basics properties of qubits, to quantum machine learning!

FAQs

What prior knowledge do I need to join?

None! We'll teach you everything, from writing basic Python to modeling qubit states on the Bloch Sphere.

Will meetings be online or in-person?

We are working on getting an in-person meeting space, but currently meetings will be held online.

What do I need for meetings?

Just bring a laptop, maybe a notebook, and a pencil for notetaking, and of course, ready your minds!.
